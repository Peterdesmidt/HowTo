How to Groom a Dog

By: Peter De Smidt

Many people have their dog sent to a
groomer even though it is just as easy
to do it at home saving them time
and money.

How to Groom A Dog

  • Brush
  • Water
  • Dog Soap
  • 5-7 Towels
  • Comb

There are many ways to wash a Dog but I will be
showing you how to wash a dog the easiest way.

Step One

First you need to get a Dog. Then you need
to have a supply of water this could be a hose,
a faucet, or even just a couple of buckets of water.
Afterthat you need to completely cover the dog in
water makeing sure that the dog is covered totally.

Step Two

After you have finished covering the dog with water
you need to lather the dog with soap makeing sure to
scrub the soap in to the fur of the dog. When lathering the
dog with soap be sure to cover the dog with an equal
amount of soap.

Step Three

After you have scrubbed the soap into the dog's fur
you want to use water and completely rinse out the
fur of the dog. While you are rinseing the fur make
scrub out all of the soap because if the soap is left
in the fur it could dry and irritate the skin on the
dog. After you have finished rinseing the dog repeat
step three two times.

Step Four

After you have completed step three three times
you should work on drying the dog off
with the towels. Depending on the size of the dog you
have tells you how many towels to use you really just want
dry the dog off until the dog is almost completely dry.

Step Five

When the dog is almost completely dry you want to use the brush
to completely brush out any knots or matted areas of the dog. You also
Want to use the comb to comb out the dog's paws. Finally you have
a clean and groomed Dog.
